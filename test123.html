World Cup 2019

The Cricket World Cup 2019 is one of the most prestigious tournament of ICC (International Cricket Council).
This year the world cup will be played in England and Wales from 30th May 2019 to 16th June 2019. It will consist of 10 teams fighting against each other for the conveted title.

This will be the first time since the commencement of the World Cup in 1975 when all the participating teams will be test playing nations.
India and England will go into the tounament as clear favorites. It is going to be an exciting summer of cricket for all the cricket lovers after the commencement of the IPL 2019.

The Biggest Rivalry to take the Centre Stage in World Cup

India vs Pakistan is considered as one of the biggest rivalries in World Cricket or any sort of sports due to the tension going on between the two countries.
India and Pakistan will lock horn in the ICC Cricket World Cup on 16th June 2019. It will be exciting to watch who emerges as victorious in this game.
